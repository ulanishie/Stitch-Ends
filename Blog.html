Ulan's Work

About the Artist and her work

I strive to have fun and promote artisan crafts, repurpose fabrics that may not be suitable to wear, into something else that will serve a purpose, and educating people on processes of sewing, and the value of handmade work as a form of art. Making clothes, but also designing what goes onto them.

"Clothes are meant to fit our bodies and personalities, not the other way around"
